School Vibe

schoolvibe.studytube.co.in

School Vibe supports school administrators, principals, and district leaders in managing institutions through data-driven insights. The platform analyzes student performance data such as test scores, internal assessments, and GPA trends to identify learning gaps and high-performing groups. The system also assists with curriculum alignment by reviewing existing teaching plans against state standards or framework requirements.

🏫 Education Management • Analytics

Concept Rozana

conceptrozana.studytube.co.in

Concept Rozana helps you master subjects through one concept each day. The app presents the concept through short video or audio lessons, followed by quizzes and practice tasks. Your learning runs in a daily streak format, which builds discipline and consistency. Each completed pack strengthens concept clarity and exam confidence through small, focused steps.

🔥 Daily Learning • Micro-Learning

Physics Coach

physicscoach.studytube.co.in

Physics Coach trains students for physics exams through structured, performance-driven practice. The system adapts question difficulty based on your performance, detects weak topics from mistakes, and shows clear analytics on accuracy, speed, and progress. Timed tests follow real exam patterns, which builds speed and confidence.

📘 Adaptive Practice • Exam Prep
